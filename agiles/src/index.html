What is Agile?

Agile is a project management methodology that emphasizes flexibility, collaboration, and customer satisfaction.

Key Principles

Agile methodologies focus on iterative development, where requirements and solutions evolve through collaboration.

Benefits of Agile

Agile promotes faster delivery, improved quality, and increased customer engagement.

Popular Frameworks

Some popular agile frameworks include Scrum, Kanban, and Extreme Programming (XP).
